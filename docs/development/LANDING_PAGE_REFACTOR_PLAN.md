## Key Messaging Principles

### Outcome-Led Copy (Not Feature-Led)
- ✅ "Sharper mornings. Deeper recovery. Better sleep."
- ❌ "Contains caffeine and L-theanine"

### Trust Signals Throughout
- Informed Sport Certified (prominent, repeated)
- Made in UK (quality signal)
- 60-Day Money-Back Guarantee (risk reversal)
- 500+ 5-star reviews, 100,000 bottles sold (social proof)

### Science After Benefits
- Sections 1-5: Outcomes and benefits only
- Section 6: Science and certification (with clear CTAs to detailed pages)
- Avoid ingredient lists in hero/product cards

## Content Requirements by Section

### Section 1: Hero
- **Headline:** Outcome-led benefit statement
- **Subheadline:** Who it's for + what it delivers
- **Stats:** "500+ 5-star reviews" + "100,000 bottles sold" + "Trusted by professional athletes"
- **Trust Badges:** Informed Sport Certified + Made in UK + 60-Day Money-Back Guarantee (visual badges)
- **CTA:** Single primary button "Shop Now" or "Find Your Formula"
- **Hero Asset:** High-quality image/video of athlete with product OR product-focused shot

### Section 2: Social Proof Carousel
- **Title:** "Trusted by Athletes and High Performers" or "What Our Community Says"
- **Review Count:** "500+ 5-star reviews" prominently displayed
- **Content:** 8-12 review cards with customer name, star rating, benefit-led quote, optional product used, optional UGC image/video
- **Filters (optional):** By benefit (Focus, Energy, Recovery, Sleep) or by product

### Section 3: Industry/Sport Credibility
- **Title:** "Trusted Across Elite Sport" or "Used by Professional Athletes In:"
- **Copy:** Single paragraph explaining usage without naming clubs
- **Visual:** Sport category icons (football, rugby, boxing, Olympic rings, racing helmet) OR silhouette athlete images
- **NO club logos** (legal constraint)

### Section 4: Product Grid
- **Title:** "Find Your Formula" or "Start Here"
- **Assurance Banner:** "60-Day Money-Back Guarantee • Free UK Shipping on Subscriptions • Cancel Anytime"
- **Product Cards:** Benefit-led headline, "best for" use cases, daily pricing, format options, CTA
- **Optional:** Trial pack card

### Section 5: High-Level Benefits
- **Title:** "What CONKA Delivers" or "Built for Performance"
- **Format:** 3-4 benefit cards with icons (brain, energy, sleep, recovery)
- **Content:** Outcome-focused headlines + 1-2 sentence explanations (NO ingredient lists)
- **Optional:** Filter/toggle by product

### Section 6: Why CONKA Works
**Section A: Formulation**
- Headline: "Formulated by Athletes, Backed by Science"
- Copy: 2-3 sentences on clinical dosing, no pixie-dusting
- CTA: "Read the Science"

**Section B: Informed Sport Certification**
- Headline: "Informed Sport Certified"
- Copy: Third-party testing, WADA compliance, zero banned substances
- Visual: Large Informed Sport logo/badge
- CTA: "View Third-Party Test Results"

**Section C: Made in UK**
- Headline: "Made in the UK to GMP Standards"
- Copy: Quality-controlled manufacturing
- Visual: UK flag or "Made in UK" badge

### Section 7: Timeline/What to Expect
- **Title:** "What to Expect" or "Your Performance Timeline"
- **Format:** Timeline graphic (horizontal desktop, vertical mobile)
- **Stages:** Week 1 (immediate effects), Weeks 2-4 (building benefits), Month 2-3 (peak performance)
- **Optional:** Toggle between Flow vs Clear timeline

### Section 8: How to Use
- **Title:** "How to Use CONKA" or "Your Daily Ritual"
- **Format:** 3-step visual process
- **Steps:** When (timing), How (straight or mix), Track (feel the difference)
- **Optional CTA:** "See Full Usage Guide"

### Section 9: Athlete Case Study
- **Title:** "Proven in the Field" or "Real Athletes, Real Results"
- **Content:** Athlete name/anonymity, sport, before/after data (reaction time, sleep, HRV), quote, data visualization
- **CTA:** "Read Full Case Study"

### Section 10: Cost Breakdown (Protocols Only)
- **Title:** "Better Value, Better Results" or "Why Protocols Work"
- **Format:** Comparison table (Individual vs Protocol)
- **Content:** Daily pricing, savings calculation, additional benefits (simplified routine, optimized ratios, consistency)

### Section 11: Community/UGC
- **Title:** "CONKA in the Wild" or "Join the Community"
- **Format:** Grid/carousel of 8-12 UGC images/videos
- **Content:** Pre-workout, unboxing, recovery, routines
- **CTA:** "Share your CONKA moment—tag @conka" or "Follow us on Instagram"

### Section 12: Risk Reversal
- **Title:** "Try CONKA Risk-Free" or "60-Day Money-Back Guarantee"
- **Copy:** No questions asked, no return shipping required
- **Sub-points:** 95% success rate, keep what you've used, cancel anytime
- **Visual:** Large badge or banner

### Section 13: FAQ
- **Title:** "Questions? We've Got Answers." or "FAQ"
- **Format:** Expandable accordion (5-7 questions)
- **Required questions:** Benefits, results timeline, athlete safety, subscription, guarantee, product differences, using both together
- **Answers:** 2-3 sentences max, link to detailed pages if needed

### Section 14: Footer
- **Newsletter:** "Get 15% Off Your First Order" with email capture
- **Standard Footer:** Links (About, Science, Athletes, Contact, FAQ, Shipping, Returns), social icons, trust badges, legal disclaimers

## Design Notes

### Mobile Optimization
- Stack content vertically where appropriate
- Swipeable carousels for reviews/UGC
- Full-width banners for guarantee
- Vertical timeline for "What to Expect"

### Desktop Optimization
- Split hero layouts (image left/right, copy opposite)
- Horizontal carousels showing 3-4 cards at once
- Side-by-side comparisons for cost breakdown
- Horizontal timeline for "What to Expect"

### Visual Consistency
- Trust badges as recognizable icons (not just text)
- Stats displayed as small icons/numbers
- Product images: High-quality assets (not white background)
- Icons: Simple, modern, consistent style
